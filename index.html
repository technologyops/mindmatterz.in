About Us

Mind Matters Academy of Life Skills has been founded with the aim of imparting quality training in Personality development, Communication, Negotiation, Interviewing, Time management and soft skills to people who have a passion for life and therefore, wish to discover their inner self and transform their personality for the better. It is our vision to integrate individuals effectively in society of which they are an inalienable part to achieve personal growth and professional success.

The logically- structured training modules; ideal for students,Job aspirants and employees in sales and service industry are designed to walk the participants through the following milestones:

  • Self awareness and personal insight
  • Dynamic goal setting
  • Self motivation & result-oriented mindset
  • Social integration & relationship management
  • Personal discovery and professional success

The training models and concepts used in this academy have benefited individuals and organizations alike with proven success and favourable return on investment.

In order to maximise the utility of the training program, Mind Matters provides customized and tailor-made modules on specific topics so as to meet the needs of the individuals and those of the sponsoring organizations.

Mr. Noel Francis, Chief Executive and Lead trainer of Mind Matters Academy of Life Skills has had over two decades of experience in the Airline industry, having worked for Pan American World Airways, Ethiopian Airlines and KLM Royal Dutch Airlines in various capacities since 1986. He has handled challenging assignments in Middle-east, Russian federation and Indian sub-continent, which included setting up KLM Cargo offices in these locations, recruitment of staff and imparting training on KRA, Commercial & negotiation skills and leadership techniques. Being a qualified psychological counsellor, he brings with him rich experience in transformation of personality, PMA, mentoring, career guidance and corporate consulting.

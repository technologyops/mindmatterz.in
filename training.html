About Training

Mind Matters Academy of Life Skills offers four courses to its participants:

  • Personality development workshop
  • Communication and negotiation skills
  • Interviewing and soft skills
  • Time management techniques

Individual participants and sponsoring organizations may choose all or any combination of these courses to suit their requirements.

The Personality development workshop is a highly recommended and interactive module replete with role plays, group discussions, videos, self-assessment tools and team-building exercises. This workshop is a perfect blend of knowledge from the fields of psychology, physiology and social science aimed at navigating the participant to a state of self-awareness, leading to self-motivation and a positive mental attitude.

The Communication & Negotiation , Interviewing & Soft skills and Time Management modules are fun and engaging, requiring a high level of participation in Role plays, Open forums, Videos and group discussions. Plain theory sessions are relegated to the minimum.

Participants, therefore will have to work in groups together with individual assignments. The progress of the participants will be reviewed by the facilitator in order to provide constructive feedback to the individual and/or the sponsoring organization.

At Mind Matters Academy of life skills, we do not perceive training to be an end in itself, but as part of a larger process that facilitates application of acquired knowledge for self development and organizational profitability.

Customized packages can be tailor-made for organizations who state their specific training goals based on a certain minimum number of participation.

The program is conducted onsite in a comfortable and fully air-conditioned environment with the best training aids and facilities for outdoor activities. We are also open to offsite training if the needs of the customer so dictate.
